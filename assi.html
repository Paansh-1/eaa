Project Assignments under Namami Gange

This web platform was developed as part of a larger project encompassing several assignments related to the Namami Gange initiative. Our team undertook the following tasks:

  • Assignment 1: Initial Research & Problem Definition - Understanding the challenges in Ganga water quality monitoring and the role of citizen participation.
  • Assignment 2: Stakeholder Analysis - Identifying key players (government bodies, NGOs, locals, industries) and their roles/needs.
  • Assignment 3: Technology Platform Proposal - Conceptualizing this web mediator platform and outlining its core features.
  • Assignment 4: UI/UX Design Mockups - Creating wireframes and visual designs for the website.
  • Assignment 5: Frontend Development (HTML/CSS) - Building the static structure and styling of the website pages.
  • Assignment 6: Frontend Development (JavaScript) - Implementing interactivity, simulations (login, slider), and dynamic elements.
  • Assignment 7: Report Submission Mechanism Design - Defining the process for organizations to submit reports (via Drive links).
  • Assignment 8: Content Creation & Curation - Writing informative text and selecting appropriate media.
  • Assignment 9: Testing & Feedback Incorporation - Ensuring cross-browser compatibility, fixing bugs, and refining based on initial feedback.
  • Assignment 10: Project Documentation & Final Presentation - Compiling all project details, team contributions, and presenting the final website.

Our role in this specific assignment (Website Development) was to bring the conceptualized platform to life, focusing on creating an appealing, informative, and functional front-end experience to encourage user engagement and facilitate the mediation process (in a simulated manner for this demo).
