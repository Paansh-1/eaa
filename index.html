Our Mission: Namami Gange

The Ganga river is not just a water body; it's a lifeline and a symbol of our cultural heritage. Decades of pollution have threatened its ecosystem and the health of millions. The Namami Gange Programme is an integrated conservation mission, approved as a 'Flagship Programme' by the Union Government to accomplish the twin objectives of effective abatement of pollution, conservation and rejuvenation of National River Ganga.

Why Join Us?

Your involvement is crucial. By reporting water quality observations or participating as a local testing organization, you become a vital link between the community and the government. Accurate, ground-level data helps authorities take targeted action. Be a part of the solution, ensure accountability, and help restore Maa Ganga to her pristine glory.

For Local Testers & Organizations

Are you a local group, NGO, or individual actively monitoring Ganga's water quality? We provide a platform to connect your valuable findings with government bodies.

Register your organization (or as an individual tester) to submit reports. We act as a mediator, ensuring your data, submitted with proper documentation via shared drive links, reaches the concerned authorities for verification and action.

Submit Water Quality Report

Please ensure your report is comprehensive and includes location details, date/time of sampling, parameters tested, results, and photographic evidence if possible. Upload your report (PDF, DOCX) to a cloud storage service (like Google Drive, Dropbox) and share the link below.

Important Notice: Accuracy is Paramount

Submitting false or misleading information regarding water quality reports is a serious offense. Such actions undermine the collective effort to clean the Ganga and can misdirect valuable resources.

Consequences of misinformation may include:

  • Heavy fines as per government regulations.
  • Denial of access to government schemes and benefits.
  • Potential legal action.

Please ensure all submitted data is accurate and verifiable. Let's work together with integrity.
